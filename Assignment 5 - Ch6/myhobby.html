The best baseball in Chicago is on the North Side

The Chicago Cubs are a historic MLB franchise and one of the most popular sports teams in the entire world. With some of the greatest fans, dominate players, and historic venue!

My Favorite Chicago Cubs Player:

Kris Bryant #17

Kris Bryant was the Cubs first selection (2nd overall) in the 2013 MLB draft. He quickly became one of the top prospects in baseball and made his major league debut in 2015. He was named an MLB All-Star and won the National League's (NL) Rookie of the Year Award! In 2016, he won the NL MVP Award and helped the Chicago Cubs win the World Series!
