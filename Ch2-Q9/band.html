    Albums

    1. Common Courtesy (2013)

      2. Common Courtesy is the fifth studio album ADTR produced. Songs for the album were written mid-2011, with recording taking place during 2012-2013. The album charted at #34 in the UK & #37 in the U.S. The best songs on the album in my opinion are: City of Ocala, Right Back At It Again, Leave All the Lights On, & Good Things.

    2. Homesick (2009)

      3. Homesick is the third studio album ADTR produced. Recording took place between October and November 2008 at the Wade Studio, with the album being later released in February 2009. The album at #21 in the U.S. and #165 in the UK. A fun fact about this album is serveral of the tracks are available for Rock Band. The best songs on the album in my opinion are: Homesick, The Downfall of Us All, & If It Means a Lot to You.

    3. What Separates Me From You (2011)

      4. What Separates Me From You is the fourth studio album ADTR produced. The album was recorded from May to July of 2010 and was later released in November 2010! The album debuted at #11 in the U.S. and the album was certified Silver in the UK by the BPI. The best songs on the album in my opinion are: This Is the House That Doubt Built & All Signs Point to Lauderdale.
